About us

Kirubel, Kire, and Mesfin are four developers from around the world. They wanted to start a social media application that would connect people from around the globe to share and celebrate their cultures. KKMS builds an application that is not only used to connect those who know each other but also those who have never met before. Our mission is to show the world that although we may be from unique areas, we bond over our similarities of love of food, entertainment, and most of all, our love for making a positive impact.
